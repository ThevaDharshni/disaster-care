Who We Are

Disaster Care Management System

A centralized platform connecting rescue teams, relief camps, and affected citizens during natural disasters.

DCMS supports government authorities, NGOs, and volunteers with shelter tracking, victim registration, resource management, missing person search, and medical records — all in real time.

Built for Tamil Nadu disaster operations, the system reduces manual coordination, speeds up family reunification, and gives decision-makers live visibility into every camp and resource.

What We Do

Core Services

Everything needed to manage disaster relief in one unified platform.

🏕️

Shelter Management

Real-time camp capacity tracking, occupancy monitoring, and facility status across all districts.

👥

Victim Registration

Register displaced individuals and families, assign them to camps, and maintain personal records securely.

📦

Resource Distribution

Track food, water, medicine, and clothing. Log every distribution with donor and recipient details.

🔍

Missing Person Search

Report missing individuals. AI-powered matching engine reunites families using name, age, blood group, and identification marks.

🏥

Medical Assistance

Record patient information and treatment notes. Monitor medical supply usage at each camp location.

📊

Reports & Analytics

Auto-generated shelter, donation, and missing-person dashboards exportable for district authorities.
